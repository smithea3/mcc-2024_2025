The summand of the given series can be rewritten as \(\displaystyle \sum_k\frac{\sin^4(k)}{k^4}\text{.}\) This proves helpful because it is also true that \(0 \leq \sin^4(x)\leq 1\text{.}\) Therefore, it is true that \(\displaystyle \frac{\sin^4(k)}{k^4} \leq \frac{1}{k^4}\) for all \(k > 1\text{.}\) Additionally, since \(\displaystyle \sum_k \frac{1}{k^4}\) is known to converge by the p-series test, then the given series must converge also by the Direct Comparison Test.
