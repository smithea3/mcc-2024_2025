Hint 4.2.3.6.1.

Note that \(\left\{\sqrt[n]{e}\right\}_k\) is monotonically decreasing and \(\displaystyle \lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{e}=1\text{.}\) So, \(\displaystyle \frac{1}{n} \leq \frac{\sqrt[n]{e}}{n}\text{.}\)
This allows you to consider either the Direct Comparison Test or Limit Comparison Test.
