We can write the Fibonacci sequence in the notation of sequences by writing \(\displaystyle \left\{ a_{k-2}+a_{k-1} \,|\, a_1 = 1, a_2 = 1 \right\}_k^\infty\text{.}\) Notice here how the first two terms of the sequence is already defined. Another important recursively-defined sequence that we will utlize in this chapter is the factorial sequence.
