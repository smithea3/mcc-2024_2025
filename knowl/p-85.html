Let \(\displaystyle \sum_k a_k\) and \(\displaystyle \sum_k b_k\) be infinite series where \(0 \leq a_k \leq b_k\text{.}\) If \(\displaystyle \sum_k b_k\) converges, then \(\displaystyle \sum_k a_k\) also converges. Contrapositively, we can say under the same conditions that if \(\displaystyle \sum_k a_k\) diverges, then \(\displaystyle \sum_k b_k\) also diverges.
