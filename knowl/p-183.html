When studying power series, and later when we apply power series to other things, we want to determine for what values of x will the power series converge. We do this by connecting it back to the geometric series. Remember that the geometric series converges when \(|r| < 1\text{.}\) So, it would be only natural that we look at the absolute convergence of the power series and determine for what values of \(x\) the power series converges absolutely.
